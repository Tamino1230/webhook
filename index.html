Is it safe?

Yes, this tool is safe to use. It does not store or log any of your webhook URLs or messages. All data is processed locally in your browser, ensuring your privacy and security.

Note: Always be cautious when using third-party tools with your Discord Webhooks. Make sure to only use trusted sources and never share your webhook URL with anyone.

What is a Discord Webhook?

A Discord Webhook is a way to send automated messages and updates to a Discord channel. It allows you to integrate external applications or services with your Discord server.

How to Use This Manager?

This tool helps you manage your Discord Webhooks easily. You can create normal messages or embed messages with custom titles, descriptions, and colors. Instead of programming it by yourself!

Why Use Embed Messages?

Embed messages allow you to format your content in a visually appealing way. They support rich text, images, and custom colors, making your messages stand out.
